I am Ruipeng Liu, a 4th-year Ph.D. student at Syracuse University, advised by [Dr. Garrett E. Katz](https://web.ecs.syr.edu/~gkatz01/?_gl=1*uya3ax*_gcl_au*MTMyMTgzOTY5Ny4xNzcyODMyMTE3*_ga*MTAzMzQ2ODU1My4xNzU1MTg5OTQx*_ga_QT13NN6N9S*czE3Nzg4MTM3NjgkbzMxJGcwJHQxNzc4ODEzNzY5JGo1OSRsMCRoMA..*_ga_SRVN5YVLB5*czE3Nzg4MTM3NjgkbzExJGcwJHQxNzc4ODEzNzY4JGo2MCRsMCRoMA..). My research interests are in neuro-symbolic AI and reinforcement learning. Prior to pursuing my Ph.D., I obtained my M.S. degree from the same institution in 2022 and my B.S. degree from Hong Kong Baptist University in 2018, during which I spent one semester as an exchange student at Dalhousie University in Fall 2016.

In addition to research, I have also served as a teaching assistant for undergraduate computer science courses, including Introduction to Analysis of Algorithms.
